Let’s just go with “designer.”

I’m a specialized generalist, using code as a tool for bringing designs to life in the browser. With a background ranging from print design to user experience to front-end development, I now spend most of my time creating design systems, digital interfaces, and online publications.

I’m currently a Staff Product Designer at Shopify, focusing on design engineering for the App and Theme Stores. I maintain our design system in both Figma and code, bridge the gap between the UX and engineering teams, and ensure that new merchant features are shipped at the highest quality.

I previously worked as a Senior Designer at GitHub, where I was part of the small team that designed, built, and maintained the marketing presence of the world’s largest open source platform. Prior to that, I was Design Lead of Release Team at Upstatement, focusing on the long-term evolution of sites for clients with limited in-house digital teams.

If you’d like to chat about bocce technique, neighborhood bears, the finer points of making homemade pierogi, or (if you must) all things design and development, don’t hesitate to say hello.
